# Search

When you know what you're looking for, you shouldn't have to scroll the [resource tree](resource-tree.md) to find it.

Press **`Cmd+K`** (macOS) or **`Ctrl+K`** (Windows/Linux) to open search, start typing a document's name, and jump straight to it. It's the fastest way to navigate a large project — keep your hands on the keyboard and go directly to the scene, chapter, or note you have in mind.
