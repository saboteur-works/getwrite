# Resource Tree

The Resource Tree is the panel that shows everything inside a project — your documents, images, audio, and folders — in a familiar, file-explorer-style layout. It's how you move around your work and keep it organized.

## Browsing and editing

Click any item to open it in the Work Area. From the tree you can create new documents and folders, rename them, and move things around — the everyday housekeeping of a writing project, all in one place.

## Reordering by drag and drop

Drag documents and folders to reorder them within the tree. The order you set isn't just cosmetic: it's the order your work appears everywhere else in GetWrite, including the [Organizer view](views/organizer.md). Arranging the tree to match the shape of your story means the rest of the app follows suit.

## What lives in the tree

The tree holds [resources](resources.md) of several kinds — text documents, images, audio, and folders that group them together. Selecting a folder opens its contents in the Work Area so you can work at the chapter or section level, not just the document level.
